Bhoomi Techzone - Web Development

Our web development timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple informational website typically takes 3-4 weeks, while e-commerce sites or custom web applications may require 8-12 weeks. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements and features needed.

We utilize modern, industry-standard technologies that ensure your website is fast, secure, and scalable. Our technology stack includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React and Angular for frontend development. For backend, we work with Node.js, PHP, and Python. We also specialize in WordPress, WooCommerce, and custom CMS development depending on your business needs.

Bhoomi The Real Estate

We offer flexible payment plans to suit various financial situations. Our standard plan includes a 10% booking amount, followed by 30% first installment within 30 days, 30% second installment within 90 days, and the final 30% at possession. We also offer construction-linked payment plans and customized plans for specific properties. Please contact our sales team for the payment options available for your preferred property.

For property booking, you'll need to provide identity proof (passport, driver's license, or government ID), address proof (utility bill or bank statement), PAN card, passport-size photographs, and bank statements or income tax returns as financial proof. For NRI customers, additional documents may be required. Our sales executives will guide you through the documentation process and help you prepare all necessary paperwork.

We provide regular construction updates to all our customers. You can check your property's construction status through our customer portal, where we upload monthly progress reports and images. Additionally, we conduct scheduled site visits for buyers, and our customer relationship managers send construction milestone updates via email. You can also contact your dedicated relationship manager directly for the latest updates on your property.

Our residential projects feature premium amenities including landscaped gardens, swimming pools, fully-equipped fitness centers, indoor games rooms, children's play areas, walking tracks, and 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance. Many of our premium properties also include clubhouses, multipurpose halls, meditation areas, and smart home features. The specific amenities vary by project and are detailed in each property's brochure and specification document.
