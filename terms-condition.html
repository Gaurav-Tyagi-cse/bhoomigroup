1. Property Bookings & Prices

Booking Terms:

Q: How do I confirm my property booking with Bhoomi The Real Estate?
A: All property bookings are subject to availability and confirmation by Bhoomi The Real Estate. A property booking is confirmed only after receipt of the booking amount as specified in the payment schedule. Property prices are subject to change without prior notice until a booking is confirmed with the required deposit payment.

Q: What happens if I delay my payment after selecting a property?
A: Bhoomi The Real Estate reserves the right to cancel any booking for which payment has not been received according to the payment schedule. To ensure your selected property remains reserved for you, it's essential to adhere to the payment timeline.

Q: In which currency are property prices quoted?
A: All prices are quoted in the currency specified in the property listing. Customers are responsible for any currency conversion charges or banking fees associated with payments.

Q: What is included in the property price?
A: The price includes only those items specifically stated in the property listing or booking confirmation. Additional services or amenities may incur extra charges. Always review the property specification document for complete details.

2. Price Guarantees and Adjustments

Property Pricing:

Q: Can the property price change after I've made the initial deposit?
A: Once a booking is confirmed with the specified deposit, the base price of the property will not change. However, government taxes, stamp duties, or legal charges may vary if changed by governing authorities before the final registration.

Q: Are there additional charges for premium locations within a project?
A: Yes, premium locations within a development project may be subject to additional charges as specified in the booking details.

  • Q: What is the premium for corner plots?
    A: Corner plots: 10% premium on base price
  • Q: Is there an extra charge for park-facing units?
    A: Park-facing units: 8% premium on base price
  • Q: Do higher floors cost more?
    A: Higher floors (above 10th): 5% premium on base price
  • Q: What about units with special views?
    A: Special view units: As specified in the booking details

Q: Can I combine multiple promotional offers or discounts?
A: Special promotional offers and discounts cannot be combined unless explicitly stated in the offer terms.

1. Payment Schedule

Standard Payment Plan:

Q: What is the standard payment schedule for purchasing a property?
A: Our standard payment plan is structured as follows:

Q: How much is the booking amount?
A: Booking Amount: 10% of the total property value is required to confirm the booking and block the unit.

Q: When is the first installment due?
A: First Installment: 30% of the total property value due within 30 days of booking.

Q: When must I pay the second installment?
A: Second Installment: 30% of the total property value due within 90 days of booking.

Q: When is the final payment due?
A: Final Installment: 30% of the total property value due at the time of possession or as per agreement.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: All payments must be made through specified banking channels, and cash payments exceeding regulated limits are not accepted as per local financial regulations.

2. Refund Policy

Property Cancellation Refunds:

Q: If I need to cancel my property booking, what refund can I expect?
A: Refund amounts for property cancellations are calculated based on the stage of the project and time elapsed since booking.

  • Q: What if I cancel within 15 days of booking?
    A: Within 15 days of booking: 90% of amount paid will be refunded
  • Q: What refund will I receive if I cancel between 16-45 days after booking?
    A: 16-45 days after booking: 75% of amount paid will be refunded
  • Q: What is the refund policy for cancellations between 46-90 days?
    A: 46-90 days after booking: 50% of amount paid will be refunded
  • Q: What happens if I cancel after 90 days?
    A: After 90 days: Refund as per agreement terms

Q: How long does it take to process a refund?
A: Processing of refunds typically takes 30-45 business days from the approval of the cancellation request. Refunds will be issued using the same payment method used for the original transaction where possible.
